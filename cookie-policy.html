2. What are cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage, and mobile SDKs that perform comparable functions. They help a service work, remember your choices, and understand how it is used.

3. The categories we use

5. Your choices and consent

Where required by law (for example, in the EU/UK), we ask for your opt-in consent before setting non-essential cookies, through a consent banner when you first visit. You can control cookies through your browser settings (to block or delete cookies) and manage app tracking through your device's privacy settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the Service works.
