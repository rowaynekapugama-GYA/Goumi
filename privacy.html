1. Who we are

goumi is a hands-free, voice-guided cooking app operated by [goumi Pty Ltd] (ABN [XX XXX XXX XXX]) ("goumi", "we", "us", "our"), based in Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the goumi app, our website at [goumi.app], and related services (together, the "Service").

goumi is intended only for users aged 18 and over. See section 11.

If you have any questions, contact our privacy contact at [privacy@goumi.app] (postal: [postal address]).

2. Scope and how this policy applies to you

goumi is available to users around the world, but we are based in Australia. The privacy laws that apply to you depend mainly on where you are located, not where we are. This policy is written to meet the standard of the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which generally also satisfies the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and its Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and gives strong protections to users elsewhere. Region-specific rights are set out in section 13.

3. The information we collect

Account and identity information — your name, email address, and login credentials. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a token and the name and email you authorise (Apple may provide a private relay email).

Health and dietary information (sensitive) — allergies, dietary restrictions, and health or nutrition goals that you choose to provide so we can tailor recipes to you. We treat this as sensitive information under the Australian Privacy Act and special category data under the GDPR, and we collect it only with your explicit, separate consent (see section 5).

Voice and audio information — when you use hands-free voice features, we process your spoken commands. Recipe and usage information — the recipes you request, save, plan, or cook; your pantry/kitchen contents; preferences; and how you interact with the Service (features used, session activity).

Device and technical information — device type, operating system, app version, language, approximate location derived from IP, and identifiers needed to run the app and keep it secure.

Age signals — where required by law (for example, in certain US states), we receive an age-range or supervision signal from the app store to confirm you are 18 or over. We use this only for age verification and compliance, and do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Payment information — subscriptions are processed by Apple and Google through their in-app purchase systems. We do not collect or store your full payment card details; we receive confirmation of your subscription status.

4. How we collect information

We collect information directly from you (when you create an account, set preferences, or speak to the app), automatically (device and usage data when you use the Service), and from third parties (your authentication provider; the app store age signal; [analytics provider] where applicable).

5. Sensitive information and explicit consent

We only collect your health, allergy, dietary, and health-goal information after you give explicit, separate consent, presented apart from our general terms. You can decline; some personalisation features may then be unavailable, but the core Service remains usable. You can withdraw this consent at any time in the app's settings, after which we will stop using that information and delete it in line with section 9.

6. How we use your information and our legal bases

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your account, and authenticate you;
  • provide the Service — generate and personalise recipes, run the hands-free voice cooking experience, and manage your meal plans and kitchen;
  • estimate nutritional information and tailor content to your dietary needs and goals;
  • process your subscription and provide customer support;
  • keep the Service secure, prevent fraud and abuse, and verify age where required;
  • comply with our legal obligations; and
  • improve the Service (using aggregated or de-identified information where possible).

Where the GDPR applies, our legal bases are: performance of a contract (to provide the Service you sign up for); your consent (for sensitive/health information, voice processing, and any non-essential analytics or communications); legitimate interests (to secure and improve the Service, where not overridden by your rights); and legal obligation (for example, age and tax compliance).

7. Artificial intelligence and automated processing

goumi uses third-party AI services to deliver its features:

  • Recipe generation is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Under Anthropic's commercial terms, the prompts and outputs we send are not used to train Anthropic's models, and operational logs are deleted on a short cycle. We minimise the personal information included in these prompts.
  • Dish images are generated by [IMAGE_PROVIDER]. We send a text description of the dish; we do not send personal information.
  • Voice transcription (converting your spoken commands to text) is handled by [STT_PROVIDER]; voice output (the app speaking to you) is generated by ElevenLabs.

These AI features generate content automatically. They do not make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you. Recipes, nutritional figures, and images are estimates and may be inaccurate — see our Terms for the relevant disclaimers.

9. International data transfers

Because some of our providers are based overseas (principally the United States), using goumi may involve transferring your personal information across borders. Where we transfer information out of the EU/UK, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses and, where applicable, the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. Australia is not currently recognised by the EU as providing "adequate" protection, so transfers of EU/UK user data to our Australian infrastructure also rely on these safeguards. We take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients protect your information consistently with this policy and applicable law.

10. Data retention and security

We keep your personal information only for as long as needed to provide the Service and meet our legal obligations, after which we delete or de-identify it. We do not retain raw voice audio beyond what is needed to process your command. You can delete your account and data at any time (see section 12).

We protect your information with measures including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, secure authentication, and restricted access to sensitive information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to meet our obligations under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme and other applicable laws.

11. Children and minimum age

goumi is an adults-only (18+) Service and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. We use a neutral age check at sign-up and, where the law requires, an app-store age signal, to keep minors out. If we become aware that someone under 18 has created an account, we will remove their access and delete their information. If you believe a minor is using goumi, contact us at [privacy@goumi.app].

12. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you have some or all of the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information (you can also edit your profile and dietary information in the app).
  • Deletion — delete your account and personal information (available in the app's settings, or by contacting us).
  • Portability — receive certain information in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Object / restrict — object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent (including for health information and voice processing) at any time.
  • Opt out of sale/sharing — for US users, we do not sell or share your information for advertising; we also honour the Global Privacy Control signal.

To exercise a right, use the controls in the app or contact [privacy@goumi.app]. We will respond within the timeframe required by your local law (generally within 30 days; up to 45 days for certain US requests). We may need to verify your identity first. Consumer health data in Washington and Nevada is also covered by a separate policy — see our Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy.
